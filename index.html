Trigger: 48-72 hours before scheduled service

Content: Appointment confirmation, preparation instructions, technician info

Benefits: Reduces no-shows, improves customer satisfaction

Post-Service Follow-ups

Trigger: 24 hours after service completion

Content: Service summary, satisfaction survey, next steps

Benefits: Quality assurance, review generation, issue resolution

Trigger: Calendar-based seasonal timing

Content: Pest activity warnings, prevention tips, service offers

Phase 1: Foundation

Weeks 1-4
  • Export customer database from current system
  • Set up Email marketing account and basic configuration
  • Create customer segments (Residential, Commercial, MBTA, BHA)
  • Design email templates matching our brand
  • Import and clean customer contact data

Phase 2: Automation Setup

Weeks 5-8
  • Build pre-service reminder automations
  • Create post-service follow-up sequences
  • Set up seasonal campaign triggers
  • Develop renewal reminder workflows
  • Test all automations with small customer group

Phase 3: Launch & Optimize

Weeks 9-12
  • Full deployment to all customer segments
